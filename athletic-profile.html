The Standard

Training alongside professional footballers

The clearest measure of any young player's standard is the company they hold. Over the past two summers, Joe has trained week-in, week-out alongside current professional footballers — same drills, same intensity, no concessions for age.

These aren't show sessions or media events. Proper closed work — technical drills, finishing patterns, possession games, conditioning blocks — set at the standard of EFL and National League first-team training. The pros set the pace; Joe matches it.

What scouts find difficult to assess from match footage alone is whether a young player can hold up at training intensity once the gap to senior football has to be bridged. The answer for Joe is that he's already been bridging it for two summers, in a group where falling off the pace doesn't go unnoticed.
