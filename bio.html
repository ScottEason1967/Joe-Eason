Biography

Bio

Who Joe is, how he plays, and what he brings to a team.

Joe Eason is an 18-year-old central midfielder from Stockton-on-Tees, currently playing senior men's football for Marske United FC at Step 5 of the English non-league pyramid. He's been playing regular men's football since age 16, developing his game against competitive opposition and gaining valuable experience across multiple seasons.

As a player, Joe is a genuine 8. Comfortable across the 6, 8 and 10, he's a two-footed technician who breaks lines with precise, weighted passes. He receives under pressure with composure, picks the right pass rather than the safe one, and controls the tempo of the game. Multiple scouts have highlighted his passing range and vision as standout qualities. His ability to read the game and position himself defensively complements his offensive capabilities, making him a complete box-to-box midfielder.

Physically, Joe is 6'2" and 84kg with elite-level athletic data verified through nine years of structured strength and conditioning work with PROformance Athlete Development. His 5m sprint (0.91s), broad jump (305cm) and Abalakov jump (62.5cm) all sit within elite benchmarks. These physical capabilities translate directly to match performance.

Tactically, Joe reads transitions early, covers centre-backs when they step forward, and supports attacks without losing defensive shape. He can operate as a ball-playing centre-back when needed. The numbers back it up: Marske concede 0.91 goals per 90 with Joe on pitch versus 2.74 without. This defensive impact is the result of his positioning intelligence, communication, and commitment to his team's shape.

Joe's leadership is consistently noted by everyone who works with him. He organises from minute one, shows maturity beyond his years, and sets standards for those around him. Scouts from Stoke City described his communication and leadership as "rare to see from young players." Charlton Athletic assessed that he "will go on to have a professional career in the game." His ability to influence the team positively extends beyond his individual performances.

Off the pitch, Joe achieved nine GCSEs at grades 7-9, served as school head boy at Red House School, holds a Duke of Edinburgh Award, and is a qualified referee. He coaches football and futsal weekly and works as an assistant strength and conditioning coach at PROformance. His commitment to development demonstrates his professionalism and character.

Joe has been scouted by nine professional clubs: Stoke City, Charlton Athletic, Middlesbrough, Sheffield United, Brighton, Shrewsbury Town, Port Vale, Oxford United and Partick Thistle. This recognition from elite academy programmes and scout networks underlines the potential that coaches and scouts see in him at such a young age.
